A Brief Introduction

Glenn Gould has been presented as one of the most prolific pianists of the 20th century.During his time and still today, he is renowned for his interpretations, performances, and recordings of Johann Sebastian Bach's keyboard compositions, most notably the Goldberg Variations. In addition to garnering international recognition for his artistry and technical skills, Gould's personality and personal life has been the subject of much curiosity.
